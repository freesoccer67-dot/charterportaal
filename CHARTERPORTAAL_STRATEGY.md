## Positioning
Charterportaal should be a private charter-control portal for mid-sized distribution companies using MendriX or Excel and fixed subcontractors.

Do not make it a generic freight exchange.

## Core workflows
- Office and charter role login
- Excel or MendriX route import
- Route review before publishing
- Private marketplace
- Route request flow
- Higher offer inside route request
- Approval flow
- Assigned routes
- Expected payment fixed by office
- Invoice amount excl. btw and incl. btw
- Disputes
- Weekly payout overview

## Design direction
Use DESIGN.md. Keep the design premium blue-white. Keep user-facing headings in Dutch.

## Pattern summary
Big platforms focus on broad freight exchange, TMS, tracking, pricing, integrations and planning. Charterportaal should focus narrower on the private daily-route charter workflow: import, publish, request, bid, approve, assign, invoice, dispute and payout.
